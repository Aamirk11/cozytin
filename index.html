Our Story

Made with love.
Every single time.

Baking has always been Hamnah's love language. What started as a way to make the people around her feel something — a warm tin for a friend, a little treat for family — quietly grew into something much more special.

At CozyTin, every cookie is made completely from scratch. Real ingredients, real time, and a lot of care. No shortcuts, no pre-packaged dough, and nothing rushed. Each batch is baked the way you'd want someone to bake it specifically for you.

The idea is simple: food made with intention just tastes different. When you open a CozyTin and it's still warm and smells exactly like it should — that's not a coincidence. That's the whole point.

You're not just getting cookies. You're becoming part of something a little warmer. Welcome to the CozyTin family. 🤎
