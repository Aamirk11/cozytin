The Beginning

Made in Chicago.
With intent.

CozyTin started with a single frustration: why was it so hard to get a genuinely great cookie in this city? Not the chain kind. Not the pre-packaged kind. The kind that's still warm, with a molten center that makes you stop mid-conversation.

I've always believed that a sweet treat can brighten someone's entire day. Baking cookies has always brought me so much happiness and comfort — and I wanted to share that same feeling with others.

Chicago was hungry for this. And we intend to keep feeding it — one small, perfect batch at a time.
